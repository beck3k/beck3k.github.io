Welcome

Welcome

JNTB Tornado going to be an app designed to help you with tornado's specifically in Indiana.

It will give you many features such as:

  • A map showing where the tornado's are
  • A location based alert system
  • Some basic safety tips
  • Some information on what the tornado might do
Tornado Tips
  • Tornado's often occur in an area commonly referred to a “tornado valley” because of cold and warm wind currents sliding past one another
  • Tornado'susually occur in the evening or at night
