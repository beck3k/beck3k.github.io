Tornado's are more serious than you think!

If you are near a tornado you should prepare

What to do:

  • Seek Shelter!
  • Try to:
    • Get to the lowest part of the structure
    • Stay away from windows, They can be sharp(which means dangerous)
    • If you can, get under a heavy object like a desk and cover your head with your arms and heads, your head is important!
    • Try to stay close to a hard wall
    • In a long term situation, its a good idea to have some food with you!

      Keep a safety kit with you in the case of an emergency!
